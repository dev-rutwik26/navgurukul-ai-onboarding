# 🎓 NavGurukul AI Engineering — Onboarding Hub

> **Welcome to the team!** This repository is your single source of truth for getting up and running as an engineer on the NavGurukul AI team.

---

## 🌍 Who We Are

NavGurukul builds **AI-driven educational products** that help underprivileged students learn effectively and accessibly. Our engineering philosophy is **Edge AI first** — pushing computation to the client side to keep costs minimal and impact maximal. Our flagship product features a Speech-to-Speech (STT/TTS) pipeline built on the Web Speech API and fine-tuned models.

## 🤝 Engineering Values

1. **🔒 Security First** — Never commit secrets. Never push directly to `main`.
2. **📦 Small PRs** — Focused, reviewable, and mergeable.
3. **💬 Communicate Early** — Raise blockers the moment they appear.
4. **🧠 Understand Your Code** — AI tools are accelerators, not replacements for comprehension.
5. **📝 Document Decisions** — Your future self (and teammates) will thank you.
